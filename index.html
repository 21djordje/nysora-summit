Learning outcomes
After the Summit, the participants will be able to:

  1. Discuss innovative, promising, and effective practices in Anesthesiology, Pain, Bariatric Surgery, Opioid-Free Anesthesia, Regional Anesthesia, Nerve Blocks
  2. Understand the advantages and disadvantages of Exparel vs. Nerve Catheters, Wallans vs. Wrist Blocks for Hand Surgery, PVB vs. ESB for thoracic surgery, OFA vs. Low-Dose Opioid, Spinal vs. General Anesthesia for Hip Fracture, and PCEA vs. PVB
  3. Identify solutions for clinical challenges
  4. Develop and expand interprofessional collaboration, and networks
